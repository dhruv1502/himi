Our blog

The Art of Choosing the Perfect Perfume

Perfumes are more than just fragrances; they reflect your personality, mood, and style. Choose scents that match your preferences and test them to experience their evolution over time. Consider the occasion and pick lighter fragrances for daytime or richer ones for evening events.

Top Perfume Trends for 2025

Perfume trends for 2025 focus on sustainability with vegan formulas, recyclable packaging, and unique ingredients like oud and saffron. Minimalistic single-note fragrances and gender-neutral perfumes are rising in demand, showcasing the industry's shift to modern preferences and values.

The Science of Fragrance: How Perfumes Are Made

Perfume-making blends creativity and science, starting with sourcing natural or synthetic ingredients, structured in layers of top, heart, and base notes. After blending with alcohol and fixatives for stability, the mixture is aged, tested, and bottled, highlighting the craftsmanship behind every fragrance.
